About Me

I'm a 17-year-old full-stack developer specializing in game development, systems programming, and creative coding. I started making games in Godot during lockdown when I was 10 years old, and over the last 7 years, I've built a technical foundation that covers everything from low-level engine architecture to high-level gameplay mechanics. I also tinker around with machine learning, IoT, and CAD. I just code for fun. I love making games and seeing my abnormal ideas come to life. Outside of programming, I often think about the complexities of life, stuff like existentialism, absurdism, and human nature.
